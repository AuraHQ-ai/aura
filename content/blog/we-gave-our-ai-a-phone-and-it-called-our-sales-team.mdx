The call itself is the least interesting part.

What matters is everything around it:

- why the call happened,
- what context it had before dialing,
- what actions happened after the transcript landed.

Without memory, voice is a gimmick.  
With memory and workflow hooks, voice becomes an execution surface.

## The stack

1. Trigger from a job or user request
2. Inject structured context into the call agent
3. Capture transcript + metadata
4. Store summary as retrievable memory
5. Trigger follow-up actions in Slack

The KPI is not "calls made."  
The KPI is "decisions advanced."
